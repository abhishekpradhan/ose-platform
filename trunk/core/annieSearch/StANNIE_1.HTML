I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I received a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering (and an M.S. in Computer Science) from Stanford University in 2001, and a B.S. in Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University.

Research

My research focuses on Web-scale information integration and data retrieval: 1) information integration: dynamic integration of myriad Web sources, and 2) data retrieval: ad-hoc retrieval of  structured data for Web-based databases.
